== Description ==

The Open Hours plugin is designed to add a convenient time schedule to your website in order to maintain it without spending too much time or energy.

The goal is to eliminate the hassle of having to go through an overcomplicated process to set up your business's operating schedule and current status.

Made with love by Pixelgrade!

== Installation ==

1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
2. Set up your schedule, by going to Customize -> Open Hours
3. Start adding either your business' schedule or the current status of your operations through our Widgets or Shortcodes
